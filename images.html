JPEG

JPG images are good for photos

GIF

GIF images are great for simple animations. However, never use them for photos. Difficult to get high levels of detail on a transparent background.

PNG

PNGs are great for anything that isn't a photo or animated. Not sure what exactly is left after taking those out of the running!? Excellent for icons, diagrams, logos, etc.

SVG

Vector based graphics!!! Resize with abandon. Great for responsive design, so probably great for mobile -> PC browser and back. Used similarly to PNG.
